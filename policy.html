Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PublicadorAutomatico ("the Application", "we", "us") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with the use of this personal developer tool operated by Gustavo Bertazzoli.

1. What This Application Does

PublicadorAutomatico is a personal automation tool that programmatically schedules and publishes content to social media platforms via their official APIs. It is operated solely by the application owner for their own personal social media account.

2. Data We Collect

This application does not collect personal data from third-party users. The only data processed includes:

  • API access tokens for the application owner's own social media accounts
  • Content data (text, images, URLs) created by the application owner
  • Operational logs for debugging purposes, stored locally

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the application owner's private server. No personal data is shared with, sold to, or accessible by third parties. No cookies or tracking technologies are used on this website.

4. Third-Party APIs

This application interacts with third-party platform APIs (including but not limited to social media and e-commerce platforms) solely on behalf of the application owner. Use of those platforms is subject to their respective terms of service and privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

Operational logs are retained for a maximum of 90 days and then automatically deleted. No personal data from other individuals is retained.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As this tool is operated as a single-user personal tool, it does not process personal data of other individuals. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us using the information below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
